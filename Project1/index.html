PhyloGalaxy

Developed by Justin Blake & Josh Montague

Controls:
[W] : Move Player Up
[A] : Move Player Left
[S] : Move Player Down
[D] : Move Player Right

Objective:
Navigate the player across the screen from left to right.
Avoid colliding with the asteroids.
Collect 5 (or more) bombs before reaching the right side.
Reach the right side with 5 bombs before the timer runs out to win.
